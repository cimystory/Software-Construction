There are three kinds of Plays :

1) EXCHANGE :

FOR EXCHANGE YOU FIRST SELECT ALL THE TILES YOU WANT TO EXCHANGE BY PRESSING THEM AND THEN PRESS EXCHANGE TILES.

2) PASS :

FOR PASS YOU SIMPLY PRESS PASS.

3) PLAY :

FOR PLAY YOU FIRST SELECT ALL THE TILES YOU WANT TO PLACE ON THE BOARD ALL AT ONCE. THEN SELECT THE SQUARES YOU WANT TO PLACE YOUR TILES ON IN THE SAME ORDER AS YOU SELECTED YOUR TILES. THEN PRESS PLAY.
